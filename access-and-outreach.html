Access & Outreach

Access programmes

For pre-university students who are intending on applying to Cambridge for entry in 2026, CUMSA offers application workshops and assistance such as:

  • Personal statement and supercurriculars workshops
  • Admissions tests workshops
  • Mock interviews
  • In-person school visits
  • Mentorship programs

These application programs are free to sign up! Look out for more details on these programs closer to the UCAS application season from June to November!

Follow our Instagram @cumsa_  to keep updated.

If you have any questions regarding applying to Cambridge, feel free to email access@cumsa.org.

All the best with your applications!
